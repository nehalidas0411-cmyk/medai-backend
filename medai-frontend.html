About MedAI Diagnostics

A free, open-source medical report analysis tool built for educational purposes. Uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to interpret medical reports and provide plain-language explanations.

🤖 AI Engine

Claude (claude-haiku-4-5) via Anthropic API — vision-capable for images, text for PDFs

🗺 Maps & Facilities

OpenStreetMap + Overpass API — 100% free, no API key required

💾 Data Storage

History stored only in your browser localStorage. Nothing leaves your device except the report for AI analysis.

🔒 Privacy

No user accounts. No tracking. No analytics. Your data is yours.

Legal & Safety Notices

⚠ Medical Disclaimer

  • This tool does NOT provide medical diagnoses or medical advice
  • AI-generated outputs may be incorrect or incomplete
  • Always consult a licensed medical professional for any health concern
  • Do not use this tool in medical emergencies — call 112 (India) immediately
  • This tool is not approved by any medical regulatory authority
  • Results should never replace clinical evaluation by a qualified doctor

🔏 Privacy & Data (DPDP Act 2023 Compliance)

  • We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data
  • Medical reports sent for analysis are processed in-memory and not retained
  • No cookies, no third-party trackers, no advertising
  • Compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
  • You may clear all local history at any time from the History tab
